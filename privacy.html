Privacy Policy

How Prodigy Plumbing handles your information — in plain English.

Effective date: July 20, 2026

Prodigy Plumbing is a family-owned plumbing company in Perris, California. This page explains, in plain English, what information we collect and what we do with it. If anything is unclear, call us at (951) 533-3014 and we'll explain it in person.

What we collect

When you call, text, or email us, we get whatever you share with us — typically your name, phone number, service address, and a description of the plumbing problem. That's it, and it comes straight from you.

How we use it

To respond to your request, schedule the appointment, prepare your quote, do and document the work, send the invoice, and follow up. Text messages are used to coordinate jobs; your carrier's standard message and data rates may apply.

We don't sell your information

Never sold, never rented. Your information is shared only when it's needed to run the business — like the Google services mentioned below — or when the law requires it.

Cookies & this website

This site doesn't run any analytics or advertising tracking today. The cookie banner stores your choice in your own browser, and nothing else happens either way. If we ever add tools like Google Analytics or Google Ads, they will load only for visitors who chose "Accept All," and we'll update this page.

One thing to know: the map on our contact page comes from Google, and like any Google embed it may set Google cookies. Google's handling of that is covered by the Google Privacy Policy.

Your choices

You can ask us what information we have about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it — except records we're required to keep, like invoices for completed work. Call (951) 533-3014 or email service@prodigyplumbing.pro and we'll take care of it.

Our services and this website aren't directed at children, and we don't knowingly collect information from anyone under 13. If we change how we handle your information, we'll update this page and the effective date at the top.

Questions?

Call or text (951) 533-3014, or email service@prodigyplumbing.pro.
