Privacy Policy

How Prodigy Plumbing handles your information — in plain English.

Effective date: July 20, 2026

Prodigy Plumbing is a family-owned plumbing company based in Perris, California. This page explains what information we collect when you contact us or use this website, how we use it, and the choices you have. If anything here is unclear, call us at (951) 533-3014 and we'll explain it in person.

What we collect

We only collect information you give us or that's needed to run a plumbing business:

  • When you call or text us — your name, phone number, service address, and details about the plumbing problem you describe.
  • When you email us or send a message through our contact form (a service provided by Tally) — whatever you include in the message, typically your name, contact details, and a description of the job. Tally processes the form and passes the submission to us.
  • From this website automatically — your cookie choice (stored in your own browser), and, only if you choose "Accept All" on the cookie banner, the analytics and advertising cookies described below. The map on our contact page is provided by Google and may also set Google cookies.

How we use it

We use your information to respond to service requests, schedule appointments, prepare quotes, perform and document the work, send invoices, and follow up on jobs. Text messages are used to coordinate scheduling and job updates; your carrier's standard message and data rates may apply.

We don't sell your information

We never sell or rent your personal information. It is shared only with service providers that help us operate — such as Tally (contact form) and the Google services described below — and, if it ever comes to that, when the law requires it.

Cookies, analytics & ads

The first time you visit, a banner asks you to choose "Accept All" or "Necessary Only."

  • Necessary Only — the site simply remembers your choice in your browser. Nothing else is tracked.
  • Accept All — allows Google Analytics cookies (which tell us, anonymously, how visitors use the site so we can improve it) and Google Ads cookies (which tell us whether our ads led to a call or visit).

Google's handling of this data is covered by the Google Privacy Policy. You can change your mind anytime: clear this site's cookies and site data in your browser and the banner will ask again on your next visit.

Your choices

You can ask us what information we have about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it — except records we're required to keep, like invoices for completed work. Call (951) 533-3014 or email service@prodigyplumbing.pro and we'll take care of it.

Children

Our services and this website are not directed at children, and we don't knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle your information, we'll update this page and the effective date at the top.

Questions?

Call or text (951) 533-3014, or email service@prodigyplumbing.pro.
