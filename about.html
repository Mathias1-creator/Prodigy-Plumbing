About us

The Fernandez family has been fixing pipes for two generations

Daniel Fernandez runs Prodigy Plumbing the way he learned the trade: do it right, charge what you quoted, and treat the house like it's your own.

Daniel's story

He didn't pick plumbing out of a catalog

Daniel Fernandez grew up in this trade. His family has worked it for two generations, and after more than 20 years running jobs himself, he founded Prodigy Plumbing in Perris to do the work on his own terms.

Those terms are simple. The price gets quoted before the work starts — and it doesn't grow while you're not looking. Corners don't get cut, because a fix that fails in six months isn't a fix. And every option gets explained in plain language — the problem, the repair, the alternatives and what each costs — so the person who owns the home is the one deciding what happens to it.

Residential or commercial, no job too big or small. If water, gas, or drainage runs through it, Prodigy handles it — from a dripping faucet in Perris to a trenchless sewer replacement across the valley.
